About the Project

Punch is a premier fitness and wellness center in Morocco, conceptualized by renowned kickboxing champion Badr Hari. More than just a gym, Punch offers a holistic environment dedicated to well-being, combining state-of-the-art fitness facilities with a community-focused atmosphere.

Project Goals & Challenges

Comprehensive service presentation – Ensured each fitness and wellness offering is distinctly highlighted with detailed descriptions.

Engaging community showcase – Developed a dedicated section to present the diverse expertise of the Punch team and foster a sense of community among members.

Responsive design – Optimized the website for seamless performance across various devices.

Design Process

Research & Benchmarking – Conducted an analysis of industry standards and user expectations to inform design decisions.

Wireframing & Prototyping – Developed wireframes and interactive prototypes to visualize the user journey.

UI Design – Crafted a modern interface that aligns with LaunchX's brand identity and emphasizes usability.

Testing & Iteration – Performed usability testing and incorporated feedback to refine the design.

Responsive Design

The design adapts seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, ensuring an optimal experience for all users.

Key Features

Diverse Training Spaces – Punch offers over 10 training areas equipped with hundreds of machines and sports equipment designed to cater to various fitness needs

Professional Coaching – A team of certified and experienced professionals is available to guide members through their training, ensuring personalized support and motivation.

Wellness Lounge – I Each Punch club features a convivial lounge offering a healthy menu and a range of curated nutritional supplements, creating a holistic wellness environment.

Exclusive Female Spaces –To meet the needs of women who prefer training in an exclusively female environment, Punch provides dedicated spaces equipped with the latest fitness and fighting technologies.

Mobile Application –Punch's digital experience includes a mobile application that allows members to book classes, schedule personal coaching sessions, access a video library, and manage in-club purchases through a wallet system.
