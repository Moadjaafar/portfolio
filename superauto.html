About the Project

SuperOccaz is an online marketplace for certified pre-owned vehicles in Morocco. The platform provides a seamless experience for both buyers and sellers, ensuring transparency, reliability, and efficiency in the used car market.

Project Goals & Challenges

User-friendly browsing experience – Designed an intuitive interface to help users easily explore vehicle listings.

Transparent pricing & details – Ensured clear information on each car with a structured layout.

Seamless online transactions – Integrated smooth navigation for purchasing, reserving, and selling vehicles.

Mobile-first approach – Optimized for a responsive experience across all devices.

Design Process

Research & Benchmarking – Conducted an analysis of industry standards and user expectations to inform design decisions.

Wireframing & Prototyping – Developed wireframes and interactive prototypes to visualize the user journey.

UI Design – Crafted a modern interface that aligns with LaunchX's brand identity and emphasizes usability.

Testing & Iteration – Performed usability testing and incorporated feedback to refine the design.

Responsive Design

The design adapts seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, ensuring an optimal experience for all users.
